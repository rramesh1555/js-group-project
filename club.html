Our Top News!

  1. WE ARE ROYALS FC

    Royals FC a 'B' division football club established in 2006 is owned by Footieculture India Pvt. Ltd. The club recognised by All India Football Federation, Karnataka State Football Association and Bangalore District Football Association, is a formidable name in competitive football tournaments having played in over 500 knockouts and leagues.

  2. POWERED BY LOVE FOR THE GAME

    The mission is to make football a premier national sport of India with a vision to spread a footballing culture and values right from the grassroots level to professional environments. The goal is to meet the aspirations of budding talent, nurture and develop them into world-class players.

  3. CREATING NEXT-GEN FOOTBALLERS

    Royals FC promotes young talent through the academy by helping the student-athletes with everything they need to conquer their ‘Football Dream’. This includes access to our professional coaches, training facilities and opportunities to leapfrog into competitive tournaments.

  4. DARED TO BELIEVE

    The idea of a Football Club was conceived by a passionate group of 16-year olds who played, dreamed and dared to believe, together. The collective thought was to give direction to our passion by playing in recognised platforms which in turn would motivate us further.

  5. THE ROYAL IS BORN

    So commenced the journey in 2006. With the intent came the name ‘Royals’, inspired by the theme of 'Rang De Basanti' a cult hit. Our young minds couldn’t help but draw parallels with football and in life where to rebel against the ordinary was imperative to become exceptional.

  6. A LEGEND IN THE BADGE

    Royals FC - Senior team brings home the coveted 'The Amateur League' (TAL) Shield

  7. INTO FIRST LEVEL

    Rajendra Singh signed a professional contract with Lonestar Kashmir FC (I-League 2nd Div.). He represents Karnataka for Santosh Trophy and participated in the reserve team trials for Jamshedpur FC (ISL).
